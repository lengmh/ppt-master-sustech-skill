- icon libraries and brand / logo icon sets

## Please review before external use

Before redistribution, commercial delivery, republishing, client delivery, or public presentation, you should verify the original license, allowed usage scope, brand rules, and attribution requirements of any third-party asset, icon, brand mark, or sourced image.

## Asset-level precedence rule

If a specific asset (image, icon, brand mark, template fragment, or other external material) carries an independent upstream license or attribution rule, that original requirement takes precedence for that asset.

## Repository-level vs asset-level scope

- Repository-level license: the default license that applies to the repository as a whole
- Asset-level license / attribution rule: the requirement attached to one specific third-party asset

Where their scopes differ, the original requirement of the specific asset should be followed.
